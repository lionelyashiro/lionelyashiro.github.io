Resume

Greetings, my name is pronounced ‘Dzerpon Lee.’ I specialize in the intersection of applied machine learning and business analytics/intelligence. My primary focus is on Geometric Machine Learning, encompassing areas such as personalized recommendations, social network analytics, target marketing, PropTech, HR analytics, financial analytics, and privacy-preserving distributed data mining.

My research aims to address significant business and managerial challenges through machine learning, predictive modeling, and computational data science. I formulate business questions as learning problems, analyze the theoretical efficacy of proposed methods, develop machine learning algorithms, and benchmark these methods using realistic data through performance evaluations, business cases, ablation tests, experiments, and other empirical analyses.
